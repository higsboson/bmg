Basics

  • What is Bemygenie?

    Bemygenie is an online portal which allows you to create and plan gift registries for any upcoming special event that you will be celebrating. There are a lot of benefits in making use of a registry for your special occasion. Click here to learn more.

  • How does Bemygenie work?

    Bemygenie allows the host to register a wishlist for the event. The host then goes on to search and add products which he/she desires to receive as gifts and add them to the wishlist one by one. Once the wishlist is created, the host will be provided with a secure link (URL) which he/she can then share with the invitees.

    The invitees will be able to access the wishlist through the URL and purchase the gists they would like to give. The gift purchased, is directly sent to the invitee by the seller. Check out our How it works section.

  • Do I need to pay anything to set up a gift registry?

    Not at all. Bemygenie is 100% free to use.

  • How many gifts can I add to my gift registry ?

    There is no limit to how big your gift registry can be.

  • Do you have a wide selection of gifts and what are their price ranges?

    We at bemygenie, offer you a virtually limitless range of gifts to choose from and our gifts come at all price ranges.

  • What special occasions can I set up a gift registry for?

    Our gift selection is ideal for Birthdays (for Kids an Adults alike), Weddings, Housewarmings, Baby Showers and for any other special event you have in mind.

  • How should I plan setting up a registry?

    It's always best to create your registry and share it with your guests a couple of weeks ahead of the speical occasion. This would give guests enough time to decide on what gifts to get you, purchase the item and have it delivered to them in time for your event.

  • Do you have any advice on how to set up a gift registry?

    Glad you asked, we are happy to help. Firstly, choose the gifts that you would love to get on your special occasion. Secondly, it's best to include gifts from all price ranges into your gift regsitry. Finally, add as many gifts as you can into your registry, so that your guests too have a choice on what to get you. For eg. if you are throwing a Birthday party, and invite 10 people, make sure you have a minimum of 12 gifts in your registry so that your guests will always have a choice.

Pricing

  • Does Bemygenie apply any addtional charge on top of the gift price?

    No. Bemygenie does not charge anything over and above the listed price of the product at the vendor site. The price shown is indicative and may change at the time of actual purchase.

  • Would the offers and discounts running on vendor site be applicable to Bemygenie user?

    The price of the product shown in Bemygenie site is only indicative. The actual price of the product is always as per the vendor site. All discounts and offers would be applicable same as when a user would have used vendor site directly.

  • The price of the product has changed since the wishlist was created. Which price will be applicable to the buyer?

    The price of product will change as per the vendor's discretion. The price applicable for buyers would the current offered price at the time of product purchase.
