Hi there, here are the frequently asked questions

Basics

  • What is Bemygenie?

    Bemygenie is an online portal which allows you to create a gift registry for any event you are planning. This helps in you not getting duplicate or unwanted gifts.

  • Where does Bemygenie gets it's gifts from?

    Bemygenie in reality is just a gift registry creator. All the products are sold by third party vendors. Most of the products are sourced and sold directly by Amazon.

  • How does Bemygenie work?

    Bemygenie allows the host to register a wishlist for the event. The host then goes on to search and add products which he/she desires to receive as gift and add them to the wishlist one by one. Once done, the host then goes on to save the wishlist. Bemygenie would provide the host a URL which he/she can share with the invitees.
    The invitees then can access the wishlist through the URL and purchase the gists they would like to give. The gift purchased is directly sent to the invitee by the seller.

Terms & Conditions

  • Will Bemygenie take any responsibility of the purchased products?

    All products showcased in Bemygenie are directly sold by external sellers through Amazon. The terms & conditions of Amazon would apply to any products purchased through Amazon portal via Bemygenie.

  • What is the return policy for gifts purchased through Bemygenie?

    All products showcased in Bemygenie are directly sold by external sellers through Amazon. The return policy of Amazon would apply to any products purchased through Amazon portal via Bemygenie.

Pricing

  • Does Bemygenie add any amount to the price of the product?

    Bemygenie does not charge anything over and above the listed price of the product at the vendor site. The price shown is indicative and may change at the time of actual purchase.

  • Would the offers and discounts running on vendor site be applicable to Bemygenie user?

    The price of the product shown in Bemygenie site is only indicative. The actual price of the product is always as per the vendor site. All discounts and offers would be applicable same as user would have used vendor site directly.

  • The price of the product has changed since the wishlist was created. Which price will be applicable to the buyer?

    The price of product will change as per vendor discretion. The price applicable for buyers would the current offered price at the time of product purchase.
