It's real simple!

Step 1: Create your gift registry


Log on to www.bemygenie.com, choose the products that appeal to you from our list, and add them to your registry. Once your list is finalized, we will host it on our site securely.

Step 2: Share the list


Share your wishlist with you guests. There are many ways to share the registry list. We recommmend you use our BMG notification system to create an "e-vite card" for your event which you can share on Whatsapp or on social media!

Step 3: Pick 'n Choose.


Your invitees will browse your registry and choose what they'd like to get for you. We make sure that once a gift is purchased then it's not avaialble for purchase by someone else.

Step 4: Delivery


The item that is chosen gets delivered to your guest. Do note that it's always best to have the registry created and shared with your guests atleast 2 weeks before the special day. That way your guests have enough time to pick a gift and have it delivered.

Step 5: Celebrate!


On the day of the event, thank your guest, unwrap your gift and have a great party!.
