Step 1


The host decides to create a gift wishlist for an upcoming event and logs onto www.bemygenie.com. Host chooses the products from the available list and adds them into the wishlist. We capture certain basic information for the host and create a unique url for the wishlist created. The wishlist url would be shown on the website and also sent via email to the host.

Step 2


The host shares the wishlist url with the invitees through email.

Step 3


The invitees who receive the email from host, click on the url provided. We will show all the products present in the wishlist for user to buy. User can click on the product thumbnail to view details about the product on third party site. User would click on the "Buy Now" button to proceed to buy the product. If purchase is completed user would click on "Purchase Completed" button on the Bemygenie popup else would click on "Did Not Buy" button. This is needed to inform Bemygenie that the gift is still not purchased and available for others to buy.

Step 4


The product is then delivered to the address provided by the invitee. This is completly taken take care of by third party and Bemygenie has no role to play in product delivery.

Step 5


Invitee then carries the product on the event date and hands over the product as a gift to the host.
